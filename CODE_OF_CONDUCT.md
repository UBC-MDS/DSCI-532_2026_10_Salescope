# Code of Conduct: DSCI 532: Group 10

# Introduction  
To ensure project success, our team prioritizes an environment of respect and open communication. We welcome diverse perspectives and encourage active learning. Every contributor is responsible for fostering a positive, professional, and inclusive culture throughout the lifecycle of this project.

This Code of Conduct applies to all project-related interactions.

# Commitment to Diversity and Inclusion  
We are dedicated to providing an inclusive environment for all participants, regardless of age, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, race, religion, or background. Our team is committed to ensuring a space where every contributor feels respected and valued, allowing everyone to participate comfortably and without fear of discrimination or exclusion.

# Expected Behaviour  
All members of this project community are expected to act with professionalism, integrity, and openness. This includes:
- Engaging with others respectfully and thoughtfully across all platforms and interactions
- Actively listening to diverse viewpoints and giving credit where it is due.
- Offering and accepting feedback in a constructive and professional way
- Participating in discussions that encourage teamwork and shared understanding

# Inappropriate Behavior  
- Any form of harassment toward participants
- Intentional intimidation, stalking, or persistent following
- Use of threats, violent imagery, or aggressive communication directed at any individual.
- Sharing or displaying offensive, explicit, or inappropriate content
- Use of slurs, excessive profanity, or aggressive and hostile language

All members are expected to refrain from all forms of unacceptable conduct to maintain a respectful environment.

# Consequences for Unacceptable Behavior  
If a participant engages in inappropriate behavior, they will be asked to stop and comply immediately. If the behavior persists or the issue is severe, it may be referred to the teaching team or course faculty. Ongoing violations may lead to removal from the project.

# Reporting Concerns and Guidelines  
If a participant violates this Code of Conduct, the incident may be reported by contacting Raghav Gupta at raghav90@student.ubc.ca. All reports will be treated seriously and handled with discretion. The Group 10 team will review the situation and take actions it considers appropriate given the circumstances.
